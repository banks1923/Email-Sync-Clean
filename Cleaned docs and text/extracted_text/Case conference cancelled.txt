Case Management Conference The above matters are called for hearing.

and four defendants have been defaulted.
Court and counsel discuss the case.
The above Order to Show Cause is discharged.
The parties are ordered to telephonically meet and confer to prepare and file a Joint
Status Update five days prior to the next appearance.

state what specific fact discovery still needs to be completed, and include a timeline
for depositions, and outline their plans for alternative dispute resolution.
Counsel are advised this Court aims to dispose of its cases within 18 months of filing
and once it sets the matter for trial, its trial dates are firm.
Counsel are further advised that when this Court does set trial dates, it can usually
set them within 90 days of a trial setting conference should the parties so desire
.
Case Management Conference is continued to 12/02/2025 at 08:30 AM in Department V
at Alhambra Courthouse.
